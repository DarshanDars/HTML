Virat Kohli (Hindustani: [ʋɪˈɾɑːʈ ˈkoːɦliː] (About this soundlisten); born 5 November 1988) is an Indian cricketer and the captain of India national cricket team in Tests.[2][3] He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League as a right-handed batsman. Kohli is often considered as one of the best cricket players in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest cricket players of all time.[4]
